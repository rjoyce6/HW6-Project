About

Designing to enrich people's lives and helping clients to achieve their goals


Bearington Studios is an architecture & design firm lead by Glenn Katz; his design ideas and solutions emphasizes in meeting the needs and aspirations of his clients and communities, while implementing innovative and sustainable solutions.

The firm was founded in 2009 and ever since, it has provided design services for both residential and commercial projects in California.

The firm has evolved to focus on creating sutainable, modern, and unique design solutions for our clients.

Services

Working actively with our customers to meet their needs.

Architecture Design

Our multidisciplinary team focuses on creating sustainable and beautiful environments for our clients. Our team is trained to help our clients to create their dream homes or commercial building.

Interior Design

Our team study helps clients to create spaces that help them to florish, improve their wellness and productivity. Our firm uses all the tools needed to create a healthy and beautiful environment the enhances our client's lives.
